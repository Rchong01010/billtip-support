 # Privacy Policy for BillTip

  **Last Updated: November 19, 2025**

  ## Overview

  BillTip ("we," "us," "our," or "the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application.

  ## Information We Collect

  ### Camera and Photo Library Access
  - The app requests permission to access your device's camera and photo library
  - This permission allows you to take photos of bills or select photos from your library
  - We do not store, transmit, or process these photos - they are only displayed locally on your device and are not sent to any server

  ### Usage Data
  - We do not collect personal data, location information, or usage analytics
  - We do not use cookies or tracking technologies

  ### Calculation Data
  - All calculations (bill amount, tip percentage, number of people) are performed locally on your device
  - This data is not stored persistently or sent to any server
  - Each time you close the app, calculation data is cleared

  ## External Services

  ### Venmo Integration
  - When you tap "Request via Venmo," the app opens a link to Venmo.com with payment details
  - This action takes you outside the app to Venmo's website or app
  - Venmo's privacy policy governs their data collection and use
  - We do not receive or store any information from Venmo transactions

  ## Data Security

  - All processing happens on your device
  - We do not collect, store, or transmit personal information
  - Your device's operating system controls access to camera and photo library permissions

  ## Children's Privacy

  This app is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

  ## Changes to This Policy

  We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date above.
